Data Structure

All data for this project was obtained from the ASO Mission in the form of raster files in GeoTIFF format. We use four raw "snowoff" rasters, which we assume are static over time. This set consists of a digital elevation map (DEM) and maps of vegetation height, canopy density (percent of a pixel covered by canopy), and land surface class (rock/soil, vegetation, water, or glacier). In addition, we use 44 "snowon" snapshots of snow depth and SWE, and a shapefile describing the basin boundaries. Both the snowoff and snowoSnow depth is provided as 3-meter and 50-meter gridded products, while SWE is available only at 50-meter resolution, due to the underlying snow density model resolution used to produce these datasets.
